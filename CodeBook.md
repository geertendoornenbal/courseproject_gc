# CodeBook for tidydata.txt
## ActivityType
The type of activity the subject was performing. Contains the following levels:
* WALKING
* WALKING_UPSTAIRS
* WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
* SITTING
* STANDING
* LAYING

## Subject
The number of the subject participating in the experiments. Contains the levels 1 - 30.
